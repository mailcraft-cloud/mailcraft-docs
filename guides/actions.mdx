# Actions

Actions are call-to-action buttons rendered in your email.

## Usage

<CodeGroup>
```typescript Node.js
mail.create('welcome')
  .to('john@example.com')
  .action('Get Started', 'https://app.example.com')
  .action('Read Docs', 'https://docs.example.com', 'secondary')
  .send()
```
```php Laravel
MailCraft::create('welcome')
    ->to('john@example.com')
    ->action('Get Started', 'https://app.example.com')
    ->action('Read Docs', 'https://docs.example.com', 'secondary')
    ->send();
```
</CodeGroup>

## Rules

- **Maximum 3 actions** per email (extras are silently dropped)
- **HTTPS required** — all URLs must use HTTPS
- **Styles**: `primary` (default, solid brand color) or `secondary` (outlined)

## Styling

Actions use your brand's primary color:
- **Primary** — solid background with white text
- **Secondary** — transparent background with colored border and text
