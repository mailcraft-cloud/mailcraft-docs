# Welcome to MailCraft

MailCraft is an open source transactional email platform for developers. Paste your website URL, we extract your brand, and you send beautiful, on-brand emails with a single API call.

**No templates. No HTML. No MJML.** AI generates the content. Your brand makes it beautiful.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Extract your brand">
    Paste your website URL. We scan it and extract colors, fonts, logo, tone, and industry using AI.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect a provider">
    Plug in your Resend API key. Your emails, your infrastructure.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send with one call">
    Call `mail.send()` with a type like "welcome" or "invoice" and your data. AI writes the copy, your layout wraps it, the provider delivers it.
  </Step>
</Steps>
